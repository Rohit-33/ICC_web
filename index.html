About Us

The ICC Music Club (Symphony) is IIT Bombay’s home for musicians and music lovers. We organize concerts, workshops, and jam sessions across all genres, nurturing talent and building a vibrant campus music community. Whether you play, sing, or just listen—there’s a place for you!
